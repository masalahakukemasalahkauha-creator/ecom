About Toyyiban Marketplace

Toyyiban Marketplace is a Sharia-compliant e-commerce platform dedicated to providing 100% Halal and Halalan Toyyiban products – not just permissible (Halal), but also pure, wholesome, safe, and beneficial (Toyyiban) as commanded in the Quran (Al-Baqarah 2:168).

100% Halal Certified

All products are verified with recognized Halal certifications, ensuring full compliance with Islamic dietary laws.

Blockchain Traceability

End-to-end supply chain transparency using blockchain technology – trace every product from source to your doorstep for complete peace of mind.

No Riba & Ethical Finance

Interest-free transactions, fair trade practices, and a portion of proceeds contributed to Zakat – fully aligned with Sharia principles.

Our Mission

We empower Muslim consumers worldwide with a trusted platform for modest activewear, natural skincare, premium dates, and more – all ethically sourced and Toyyiban assured.

Why Toyyiban?

Inspired by Islamic teachings, we go beyond basic Halal to ensure products are nutritious, sustainable, and beneficial for body and soul.
